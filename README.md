# US Aviation Trends Analysis 2023

📌 **Purpose of the Project**

This project analyzes US civil flight data from 2023, with a focus on canceled and delayed flights. The main objectives are:

* To identify the largest aircraft manufacturer and airline operating in the US in 2023.
* To uncover patterns in flight cancellations by month, day, time, and route.
* To analyze the relationship between aircraft age and departure delays.

---

🔍 **Analysis Performed**

1. Data Cleaning & Preparation (6.7M rows across 2 datasets)
2. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on flights and disruption data
3. Cancellation Analysis by airline, month, day, and departure time
4. Departure Delay Analysis by airline and aircraft age
5. Route Analysis for top 10 most canceled routes
6. Aircraft Manufacturer Market Share Analysis

---

📌 **Results**

* Boeing holds 46% of the US aircraft market, making it the dominant manufacturer.
* Southwest Airlines Co. is the largest US airline by number of flights operated and canceled.
* July had the highest flight cancellation rate; November had the lowest.
* Tuesdays had the highest cancellation rate; Fridays had the least.
* Afternoons had the highest cancellation rate at 31.1%.
* The most canceled route was Boston Logan Airport (BOS) to LaGuardia (LGA).
* 89.7% of canceled flights were short-haul.
* Aircraft age showed no clear correlation with departure delays.
